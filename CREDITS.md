# Credits

This dotfiles repository contains configurations inspired by or directly sourced from various open-source projects and contributors on GitHub. Below is a list of the main sources and credits:

## Main Sources

### JaKooLit (https://github.com/JaKooLit)
- **Waybar configurations and styles**: Majority of the waybar configs, styles, and scripts are from JaKooLit's dotfiles.
- **Hyprland scripts**: Various scripts in `hypr/scripts/` are sourced from JaKooLit's Hyprland-Dots repository.
- **Rofi configurations and themes**: Many rofi configs and themes are from JaKooLit's dotfiles.

### mrbeardad (https://github.com/mrbeardad)
- **Neovim configuration**: The nvim config in `nvim/` is based on mrbeardad's LazyVim-based setup.

### LazyVim (https://github.com/LazyVim/LazyVim)
- **Neovim base**: The nvim config is built on top of LazyVim.

### gbprod/yanky.nvim (https://github.com/gbprod/yanky.nvim)
- **Neovim plugin**: Used in the nvim configuration for yank history management.

## Additional Credits

### DevNChill (https://github.com/DevNChill)
- **Waybar EverForest style**: The EverForest theme for waybar is designed by DevNChill.

### Krautt (https://github.com/Krautt)
- **Waybar styles**: Several waybar styles including Crystal Clear, Simple Pink, Crimson, Golden Noir, and Purpl are designed by Krautt.

### adi1090x (https://github.com/adi1090x)
- **Rofi themes**: Various rofi themes (KooL_style-2-Dark, KooL_style-4, KooL_style-7, KooL_style-14) are based on adi1090x's rofi configurations.

### arkboix (https://github.com/arkboix)
- **Rofi solarized theme**: The KooL_style-15-solarized theme is based on arkboix's dotfiles.

### newmanls (https://github.com/newmanls)
- **Rofi Win11 themes**: The KooL_style-11-Win11 themes are sourced from newmanls.

### lonerOrz (https://github.com/lonerOrz)
- **Rofi themes**: KooL_LonerOrZ and KooL_style-9 themes were submitted by lonerOrz.

### JosephArmas (https://github.com/JosephArmas)
- **Rofi calc config**: The calculator configuration for rofi was submitted by JosephArmas.

### Tomaszal (https://github.com/Tomaszal)
- **Rofi current theme**: The current.rasi theme was created by Tomaszal.

### Dave Davenport (https://github.com/DaveDavenport, https://github.com/davatorium/rofi)
- **Rofi modifications**: Modifications to the rofi current theme by Dave Davenport.

### 791E (https://codeberg.org/791E/dotfiles)
- **Hyprland configuration**: Hyprland configs are sourced from 791E's dotfiles.

### Base16 Material Color Scheme (https://github.com/ntpeters/base16-materialtheme-scheme)
- **Rofi theme base**: The rofi current.rasi is originally based on the Base16 Material color scheme.
